Almaty international Airport - the best airport in the world.

Almaty international Airport is a major international airport 15 km (9.3 mi) northeast of Almaty, the largest city and commercial capital of Kazakhstan. It is the busiest airport in that country, accounting for half of passenger traffic and 68% of cargo traffic.
